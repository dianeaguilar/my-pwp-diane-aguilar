PWP Milestone One

Purpose, Audience, & Goal

Purpose:

The purpose of this project is to create a personal profile website showcasing front-end web design and development focusing on professional networking and full time employment.

Audience:

The target audience consists of Employers, Recruiters, Associates, Clients, and other Web Developers in a professional manner. This audience would allow for broader networking from employment opportunities to web development collaborations.

Goal:

My goal is to build a professional network by offering my target audience a channel to connect with me while also representing my portfolio.

Persona

Name: Mark Dawson

Profession: Lead Developer at Nature Balance

Nature Balance is a Plant-Based Diet company specializing in fruits and vegetables diet supplements. Mr. Dawson is in need of a Web Developer to join his team and contribute to the quality, service, and positive awareness of Nature Balance's products.

Technology

  • Devices: Apple MacBook Pro 16, Iphone 12 Pro Max,IPad Air OS 14, Apple Watch Series 6
  • Proficiency: Superior Power User
  • Passion for technology: Extreme

Attitudes and Needs

Looking to hire someone who will do whatever it takes and go above and beyond. The perfect candidate would be proficient in HTML, CSS, PHP,JavaScript, and JQuery. The applicant should have good critical thinking skills, work well in a team environment, and be detail-oriented. Mark is looking for someone with 2 years experience and has a good understanding of information architecture.

Frustrations

As business is growing, with more products being introduced, Mark's current team is overloaded. He has been very busy with other projects and cannot seem to make time to assist his team. Mark has a salary cap, and will have to hire a Web Developer within an given allowance. He anticipates on hiring someone within the next 2 weeks, although he is having a hard time finding someone with experience.

Goal

Nature Balance would like to have the position of Web Developer to be filled within two weeks in order to boost sales and service.

Role

Proficient User

User Story:

Mr. Dawson would like to hire an experienced web developer within the next 2 weeks, to assist with his current team's workload.
