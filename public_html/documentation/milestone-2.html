Content Strategy

My Personal Portfolio will incorporate each of the following sections: Welcome, About Me, Portfolio, and Contact Ablility.

Welcome:

A brief introduction highlighting my purpose and goal of the Professional Portfolio Website.

About Me

This section will describe my strengths, skills, and work ethic. It will also incorporate how they would be applied as a Fullstack Web Developer in relation to interested employers or other developers wishing to collaborate. Image Icons will be included beneath About Me section indicating programming languages, tool kit, operating systems, version-control and collaboration platform.

Portfolio

This portion will showcase the top three projects I have completed, as part of the Deep Dive Fullstack Bootcamp. When a project is clicked on it will open up for a closer look at the project with a description.

Contact

This portion will contain a modal button, and when clicked, a pop up will appear with a message box providing text input boxes for anyone who wishes to contact me. The input boxes are as follows: Your Name, Your Email, Your message. A send button will be attached to this section, which would send the information provided to myself, via email.
